General Advice

Why Preparation is Your Biggest Confidence Booster

Focuses on the "First Day Factor"

Forgetting essentials such as proper socks or goggles can lead to stress and discomfort. Proper preparation ensures you have everything you need for a successful day on the slopes, boosting your confidence and enjoyment.

Should I Rent or Buy? A Quick Guide

Rent if: It's your first 1-3 times, you're still growing, or you only ride once a year.

Buy if: You plan to ride regularly (5+ times per season), you've found a style of board you love, and you want consistent performance.

Quick Tips for Your First Day

Take a Lesson: Invest in a 2-hour lesson; it saves you days of frustration on the slopes.

Start Slow: Stick to the beginner 'green' runs all day

Yield to Downhill Riders: The person below you has the right of way.

Don't Stop: Never stop on the slope where people can't see you. Move to the side immediately.
