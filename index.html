SLOPE READY: YOUR DAY-ONE DONE RIGHT

Getting ready for your first snowboard trip can feel overwhelming. You're nervous about the mountain, and you shouldn't have to worry about your gear, too. Slope Ready tells you exactly what you must bring for a comfortable and confident introduction to the mountain. We remove all preparation guesswork for new riders so they can focus entirely on having the best possible first day.

Stop Guessing, Start Shredding. Your first day on the snow is the most important one. We break down the complicated world of gear into two simple categories: Core Gear (Must-Have) and First-Time Essentials (Don't Forget!), ensuring you are prepared, warm, and confident when you strap in.
